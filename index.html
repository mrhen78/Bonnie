Remembering Bonnie

The Life of a Boston Terrier

July 1, 2007 - January 21, 2016

Gone But Not Forgotten

Click on the Images Below to See Bonnie's Final Years

Bonnie was an exceptionally kind-hearted and obedient Boston Terrier. She never barked or made any noise--unless she was playing. She grew up with the Dinovo family, and she was Charlotte's girl for the first six years of her life. When Charlotte's brother was born, he was allergic to Bonnie, so she had to go live with Matt, a friend of the family. Bonnie had a happy life with both families. Her life was sadly cut short when a rare form of cancer was discovered in her chest. In spite of the best efforts put forth to save her, Bonnie passed away far too early at the age of 8. She is still rememebered and still loved. This website is devoted to her final years.

Bonnie is best remembered as being...

  • Very nuturing
  • Very sweet
  • Very quiet
  • Very laid back
  • A little kid (she thought she was one)
